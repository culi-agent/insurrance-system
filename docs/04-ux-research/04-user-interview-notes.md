# User Interview Notes - Ghi Chú Phỏng Vấn Người Dùng

---

## 1. Interview Protocol

### 1.1. Interview Structure
| Section | Duration | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Warm-up & Background | 5 min | Build rapport, demographics |
| Current Insurance Experience | 15 min | Pain points, behaviors |
| Product Concept Testing | 15 min | Reaction to Insurance System |
| Prototype Walkthrough | 15 min | Usability feedback |
| Wrap-up & Open Questions | 10 min | Final thoughts |

### 1.2. Screening Criteria
- Age: 22-55
- Income: > 10M VND/month
- Location: HCM or Hanoi (urban)
- At least 1 of: Currently has insurance / Interested in buying / Recently bought

## 7. Behavioral Observations (Prototype Testing)

### 7.1. Navigation Patterns
- 80% started by browsing categories (not search)
- 70% went straight to "Xem giá" without reading product details
- 60% compared before reading individual product pages
- 90% scrolled past long text blocks without reading

### 7.2. Common Usability Issues

| Issue | Severity | Frequency | Solution |
|-------|----------|-----------|----------|
| Quote form too long | High | 8/12 users | Progressive disclosure |
| Unclear CTA hierarchy | Medium | 6/12 users | Primary/secondary button styling |
| Health declaration intimidating | High | 7/12 users | Plain language, tooltips |
| Can't find exclusions | High | 9/12 users | Dedicated tab, not hidden in T&C |
| Payment options not obvious | Medium | 5/12 users | All logos visible upfront |
| Comparison table too wide on mobile | High | 10/12 users | Horizontal scroll + sticky headers |

### 7.3. Positive Reactions

| Feature | Reaction | Quote |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Multi-insurer prices | 😍 Delight | "Ồ, thấy ngay giá tất cả!" |
| AI recommendation | 😊 Interested | "Hay đó, khỏi tự tìm" |
| Instant policy after pay | 😊 Surprised | "Nhanh vậy luôn hả?" |
| Claims timeline | 😌 Relieved | "Thấy được process thì yên tâm" |
| Plain language tooltips | 😊 Appreciated | "À, ra vậy. Giờ mới hiểu" |
