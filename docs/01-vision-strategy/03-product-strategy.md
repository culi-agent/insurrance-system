## 3. Go-to-Market Strategy

### 3.1. Target Market Sizing

```
TAM (Total Addressable Market): $15B
├── Life Insurance: $8B
├── Non-life Insurance: $5B
└── Health Insurance: $2B

SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market): $3B
├── Online-capable customers: ~20% of market
└── Digital-first segment

SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market): $150M
├── Year 1-3 realistic capture
└── 5% of SAM
```

### 3.2. Customer Acquisition Strategy

#### Channel Mix
| Channel | Budget % | Expected CAC | Expected Volume |
|---------|----------|--------------|-----------------|
| SEO/Content Marketing | 25% | 50k VND | High |
| Google Ads (SEM) | 20% | 150k VND | Medium |
| Facebook/Instagram Ads | 20% | 120k VND | High |
| Referral Program | 15% | 80k VND | Medium |
| Partnerships (Banks, Fintechs) | 10% | 100k VND | Medium |
| Offline Events | 5% | 200k VND | Low |
| PR/Media | 5% | 30k VND | Low |

#### Acquisition Funnel
```
Awareness (1,000,000 visitors/month)
    └── Interest (300,000 - 30% CTR)
         └── Consideration (90,000 - 30% engagement)
              └── Intent (27,000 - 30% quote request)
                   └── Purchase (5,400 - 20% conversion)
                        └── Retention (4,320 - 80% renewal)
```

### 3.3. Pricing Strategy

#### Revenue Model
1. **Commission Income** (70% of revenue)
   - Commission từ insurer: 15-40% of premium tùy sản phẩm
   - Average commission rate: 25%

2. **Platform Fee** (15% of revenue)
   - Transaction fee cho high-value policies
   - Premium processing fee

3. **Value-added Services** (10% of revenue)
   - Claims assistance fee
   - Policy comparison report (B2B)
   - Data analytics services

4. **Advertising** (5% of revenue)
   - Featured listing cho insurers
   - Sponsored recommendations

## 4. Competitive Analysis

### 4.1. Direct Competitors

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Market Share |
|-----------|-----------|------------|--------------|
| PVI Direct | Brand, legacy customers | Poor UX, limited products | 15% |
| Liberty Online | International brand | Limited localization | 8% |
| Bảo Việt Online | Largest insurer, trust | Slow, bureaucratic | 20% |
| Saladin | Tech-first, good UX | Limited products, new brand | 3% |
| Tiki Insurance | Large user base | Not core business | 2% |

### 4.2. Competitive Advantages

1. **Platform play**: Không phải insurer, nên objective trong tư vấn
2. **Full-stack digital**: End-to-end online experience
3. **Data advantage**: Cross-product data cho better recommendation
4. **Speed**: Instant quote, instant issuance
5. **Multi-insurer**: Best price guarantee

## 5. Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Regulatory changes | Medium | High | Legal team, compliance first approach |
| Insurer partner churn | Low | High | Multi-partner, value-add services |
| Customer data breach | Low | Critical | SOC2, ISO 27001, encryption |
| Market downturn | Medium | Medium | Diversified products, cost control |
| Technology failure | Low | High | Multi-cloud, disaster recovery |
| Competition from incumbents | High | Medium | Speed of innovation, UX superiority |

## 6. Success Metrics & OKRs

- KR1: Launch MVP within 3 months

- KR1: Brand awareness > 5% in target segment

**Objective 3: Establish partnerships**
- KR1: Sign contracts with 5+ insurance companies
